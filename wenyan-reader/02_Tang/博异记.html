    Preface to the Record of Strange and Marvelous Things

    Indeed, the practice of discussing strange and marvelous things has been long established. Without broad knowledge and strong memory, how could one know it? However, one must record and know the true and false versions of such stories, as their origins and developments are all present. To determine their true nature, the names and surnames are without error. Having understood the discussions of the talented, it is also the rhythm of the guests. If compiled and completed, it will flow smoothly in response. I, at my western study, serving in the northern capital, upon recalling past events, would then discuss and compile, completing it into a volume. Not only to provide laughter and conversation, but also to roughly reveal admonitions. Perhaps hoping that words that may be unpleasant to hear will slightly gain warnings for self-preservation. Only seeking to align with oneself, why bother with a title? This is called "Gushenzi."

    Imperially Approved Collection of Books
    Bo Yi Ji
    Respectfully Presented by Jing Yuan Ying

    During the Tianbao era, there was a man named Chen Zhong Gong who lived in Jinling and possessed a great deal of wealth. Chen Zhong Gong loved learning and refining his writing but had not yet succeeded. He then carried several thousand pieces of gold to Qinghua Li in Luoyang, where he rented a house. The well in this house was particularly deep and had a tendency to drown people. Chen Zhong Gong knew about this but was not deterred, as he had no family and no one to fear for. He often copied and studied without leaving the house for more than a month.

    One day, a girl from a neighboring household, about ten or so years old, came to the well every day and stayed there for a long time. Suddenly, she fell into the well and drowned. The well was very deep, and it took all night to retrieve her body. Chen Zhong Gong was surprised by this and, taking a break, looked up at the well. Suddenly, he saw a girl's face in the water, young and beautiful, dressed in the fashion of the time, and looking at him. Chen Zhong Gong stared intently, and she smiled, revealing a red sleeve half covering her face. Her charming and alluring appearance was beyond the world. Chen Zhong Gong's spirit was so captivated that he could barely stand. He sighed and said, "This is the reason why people drown here." He then turned away and left.

    A few months later, during a severe drought, the well did not dry up. One morning, a man knocked on the door and said, "I am Jing Yuan Ying and request an audience." Chen Zhong Gong invited him in, and he was the same girl he had seen in the well, dressed in red and green clothing, with a lead-like adornment.
