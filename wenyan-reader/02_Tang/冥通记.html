    便下席，未出户，见门上有令春、刘白等（令春是姨母间裸子，刘白是白从子），乃又曰：“勿令小儿辈逼坛靖，靖中有真经，前失火处，大屋基，今犹有吏兵防护，莫轻洿慢。其辈无知，事延家主，卿姨病源乃重，虽不能致毙，亦难除。”子良因问：“不审若为治疗？股中又有结病，何当得除？”答曰：“不可即除，岁月之间不知若为耳。腹中亦有卒可差，别当向卿言。”令春等去，便下阶而灭。

    　　二十七曰二更中，开眼见一人，在床前，容质端正，有须，须甚厚，细眉目，年可二十余，颜状甚可爱。著芙蓉冠，垂青缨甚长，著衣状如单衣，而有朱青黄白相杂厕，似锦复非素。腰带不知是何所著，亦有光，如前范帅来时烛光也。独自而已，自云是中山人，因言曰：“茅君用尔为丞，已遣丞帅来相报。事已定，吾今来教尔修道之方。可从而言疏。”子良仍起，襞纸疏之。

    He quickly descended from the mat, not yet out the door, and saw on the door Mr. Lingchun, Mr. Liu Bai, and others. Lingchun is the son of the aunt, and Liu Bai is the grandson of Bai. He then said, "Do not let the young ones approach the altar of tranquility. The altar contains true scriptures. The place where there was a fire before, the foundation of the large house, is still guarded by officials and soldiers. Do not treat lightly or disrespect. They are ignorant, and their actions will affect the master of the house. Your aunt's illness is severe, though it may not be fatal, it is difficult to cure." Zi Liang asked, "I wonder how to treat it. There is another lump in the leg. When will it be removed?" He replied, "It cannot be removed immediately; it is unclear what will happen over time. There is also a sudden improvement in the abdomen, which I will tell you about separately." After Lingchun and others left, he descended the steps and disappeared.

    On the twenty-seventh day, during the second watch of the night, he opened his eyes and saw a person in front of the bed. The person had a dignified appearance, a beard, a thick beard, and fine eyebrows and eyes. He was around twenty years old, and his appearance was very lovely. He wore a peony cap with long green tassels. His clothes were like a simple robe, with red, blue, yellow, and white mixed together, resembling brocade but not plain. The belt was unknown, but it also had light, similar to the candlelight when the previous commander came. He was alone. He said he was from Zhongshan. He then said, "Master Mao has appointed you as a deputy and has already sent a deputy commander to inform you. The matter is settled, and I am here to teach you the way of cultivation. You can write it down." Zi Liang then stood up and wrote it down.
