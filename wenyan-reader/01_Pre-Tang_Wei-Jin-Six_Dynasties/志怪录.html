    Recording strange events may not be as beneficial as recording ordinary matters, yet mysterious and strange occurrences are an inevitable part of the universe. Unusual events are beyond ordinary human thought and consideration. If one can record the reality of such events, then those who encounter them suddenly will know how to avoid or evade them, and such records can serve as a means of encouragement and punishment, thus having some benefit. Moreover, such stories can be eye-catching and alarming, and they are enjoyable to discuss and hear. In the past, Hong's Yijian Zhi consisted of 420 volumes. If he did not find joy in it, why did he continue writing for so long? I understand that my book, though vulgar and crude, cannot compare with Hong's work, but I am simply following my own interests.     吾乡孔公名镛，字韶父，宣圣五十八代孙也。人忠孝弘毅，行巳唯以一诚可以质诸神明，不忝圣冑，乡邦增重久矣。平生政绩不可胜书。今岁己酉之秋，以都御史自贵州巡抚召工部侍郎，行至杭之富阳驿，少驻命驿吏视风色，忽觉瞑眩，从者急扶入舟，遂奄然而薨焉。将薨而有火起于舟上，甚大而烈，暨公之气渐微则火光渐高而渐小，迨于气绝则光巳在天际凝定烨煜，宛然一星矣。余谓自传相之后，载籍中贤臣多矣，而未闻有踵骑箕之列者，今而得公，殊足钦仰。穪之孔镛星可矣，余纂怪牒濒竟得此，遂谨以尾吾书，以览者假重于公焉。
