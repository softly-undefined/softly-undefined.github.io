Wenyan Reader

The following are a collection of AI translations from Classical Chinese to English. Translations on this site shouldn't be considered reliable translations of the source language. Rather, we see AI as a tool in allowing users to find the texts, without spending excessive time attempting to understand complex Classical Chinese texts. Through markers in the original and translated text, the source Chinese is consistently tied to the translation. Upon finding a passage, the user can conduct a further close reading of the passage using the explanation feature.

Created by an interdisciplinary team at the University of Maryland, College Park from the Department of East Asian Languages and Cultures and the Computational Linguistics and Information Processing CLIP Lab in the Department of Computer Science.
