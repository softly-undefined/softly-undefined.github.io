              Hi! I'm Eric, a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at
              Northeastern University advised by
              Terra Blevins and
              David Smith.
            

            

              My research is in multilingual Natural Language Processing (NLP).
              Language translation is an interpretive act, and as AI is
              increasingly being trusted for that interpretation, I'm
              passionate about ensuring translations are faithful and useful to
              humans. More broadly, as language model-based tools increasingly
              spread through society, I am interested in how we can make sure
              their benefits extend beyond a handful of languages.
              Specifically, I focus on:
            

            
    
              
  • 
                Using linguistic relationships between languages to inform the
                development of multilingual NLP systems.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Using human-centered evaluation to address the gaps between the
                perceived and actual performance of these systems.
