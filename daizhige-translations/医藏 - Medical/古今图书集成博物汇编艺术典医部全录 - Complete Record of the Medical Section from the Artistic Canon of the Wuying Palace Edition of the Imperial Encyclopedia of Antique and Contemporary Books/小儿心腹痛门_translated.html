Abdominal Pain

If a child experiences abdominal pain, has a thin body, pale complexion, lacks luster in the eyes, has cold breath in the mouth, has no desire to eat, or is vomiting with foul breath, this indicates a deficiency in the spleen and is harmed by cold water. Use Yihuang San (Enhanced Yellow Powder) or Tiaozhong Wan (Regulating the Middle Pill) to treat it. If the breath is warm in the mouth, complexion is yellow and white, lacks eye luster, or if there is excessive sleep with poor appetite or foul-smelling stools, this is due to accumulated pain, and use Xiaojiyuan (Pill for Digestion) to treat it. If severe, administer white cakes (Bai Bingzi) followed by Baizhu San (Atractylodes Powder) to tonify the stomach energy.

Note: Abdominal pain with no appetite, cold breath, diarrhea with foul breath, etc., belong to the category of physical illness caused by deficiency and cold. Use Liu Jun Zi Tang (Six Gentlemen Decoction) with roasted ginger for warming. Symptoms such as excessive sleep, warm breath with poor appetite, yellow and white complexion, etc., indicate an excess of pathogenic qi yet deficiency of original qi. Use Liu Jun Zi Tang with Shen Qu (Malt) and Shan Zha (Hawthorn) for digestive assistance.[1p]
